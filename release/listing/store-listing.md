## Full description  (≤ 4000 chars)
```
Azure Standard (Unofficial) is an independent, ad-free convenience app that opens the real azurestandard.com store in a fast, native shell — so you can browse, sign in, fill your cart, and check out right from your phone.

⚠️ Not affiliated with Azure Standard. This is an unofficial, personal-convenience app. It is not made by, endorsed by, or connected to Azure Standard. "Azure Standard" and its marks belong to their owner and are used only to describe what the app opens. The app does not use their logo.

WHAT IT DOES
• Opens the genuine azurestandard.com store in a native WebView — nothing is faked, scraped, or proxied.
• Your login, cart, checkout, orders, and payments all happen on Azure Standard's own site, under their terms and privacy policy.
• Optional push notifications — opt in for handy reminders like a drop-date nudge.
• Works with the store's built-in barcode scanner (asks for camera permission only when you use it).

PRIVACY-FIRST
• No ads. No analytics added by the app. No selling of data.
• The app never reads or stores your Azure Standard credentials, cart, orders, or payment details.
• Full policy: https://azurestandard.atsumilabs.com/privacy

WHO IT'S FOR
Anyone who already shops at Azure Standard and wants a quick, app-like way to reach the store from their home screen.

This app simply provides a convenient window to a website you could also open in any browser. For anything about your orders or account, contact Azure Standard directly.
```
