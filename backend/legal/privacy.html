Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This app is unofficial and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Azure Standard. It is a personal convenience app that opens the real azurestandard.com store inside a native shell (a WebView). This policy covers the wrapper app itself — your actual shopping is governed by Azure Standard’s own privacy policy.

Your shopping data isn’t ours

When you browse, sign in, fill a cart, or check out, you are using azurestandard.com directly inside the app’s WebView. Your account, order, address, and payment information are handled by Azure Standard under their own systems and privacy policy. The wrapper does not intercept, read, store, or transmit your store credentials, cart, orders, or payment details.

What the wrapper itself handles

Push notifications (optional). If you allow notifications, the app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging and a device push token so it can send reminders (for example, a drop-date nudge). The token identifies your device install, not you personally, and is stored on our server (azurestandard.atsumilabs.com) only to deliver these notifications. It is never sold or shared. If you deny or disable notifications, it isn’t used.

Camera (only for the store’s barcode scanner). The Azure Standard website has a barcode scanner for looking up products. If you use it, the app grants the website access to your camera so it can read a barcode. The app itself does not capture, store, or transmit any photos or video — the camera feed is used live by the website and nothing is retained by the wrapper.

Advertising ID. The Android build declares the advertising-ID permission to leave room for ads in a future version, but no ad SDK ships today and the advertising ID is not accessed or collected. If that ever changes, this policy and the store’s data-safety disclosures will be updated first.

No wrapper analytics. The wrapper adds no analytics or tracking of its own on top of the website.

What we do not collect

  • No Azure Standard account credentials, cart, orders, or payment data.
  • No photos or video from the camera.
  • No advertising profiles, and no selling of any data.

Children

This is a general-audience personal app and is not directed at children under 13.

Your choices

You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings, and revoke the camera permission there too. Removing the app removes its local data; your Azure Standard account is managed on azurestandard.com.

Changes

We may update this policy; when we do, we’ll change the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this app? Email contact@atsumilabs.com. For anything about your orders or account, contact Azure Standard directly.
